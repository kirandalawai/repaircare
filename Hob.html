Welcome to Hob Repair Care

In a kitchen the hob is a projection, shelf, grate or bench for holding food or utensils at the back or side of a hearth (fireplace) to keep them warm, or an internal chimney corner. In modern British English usage, the word refers to a cooktop or hotplate, as distinguished from an oven.

When your gas stove or hob require repair it is most certainly advisable to hire a professional gas stove or hob repair service to rectify the problem as an improperly repaired gas stove can be a fire hazard.

A regular cleaning, maintenance and service schedule for your gas stove and hob will reduce the chances of a breakdown. If you find yourself with a malfunctioning gas stove or hob and are looking for a gas stove or hob repair service, it is best to start by asking friends, family and neighbours for references of local gas stove or hob repair services they may have used in the past and were satisfied with.

Follow up by calling the gas stove or hob repair services on your shortlist and asking if they deal with your particular problem, describe the malfunction in detail, providing the brand and model number of your gas stove or hob if possible and inquire if they provide original parts and the charges for a visit to inspect the appliance that is malfunctioning. A professional gas stove and hob repair service will offer satisfactory answers to all your queries regarding parts etc., as well as providing transparent pricing information for the visit, labor and spare parts costs.
